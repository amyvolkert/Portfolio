Bringing a Strong Approach to Problem Solving

As a full-stack developer with project management experience, Amy Volkert brings a creative, solid approach to the design, development, and implementation of various web applications.
After graduating in Technical Journalism with a concentration in English from Colorado State University, Amy began her career as a research and database manager in the publishing industry. She quickly found her niche in instructional design, producing computer- and web-based learning simulations for a variety of industries and clients. She was regularly recognized by superiors for outstanding performance. One client selected her work as a standard for courses and another's satisfation resulted in three subsequent projects.
Amy became interested in web development during her collaboration with graphic artists and computer programmers. In October 2017 she completed a comprehensive 24-week-long program dedicated to designing and building web applications. Amy lives in Denver, Colorado, with her husband, two teenage children, three dogs, and a cat.
